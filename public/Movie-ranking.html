The Best Movies According To Sofja

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Kon Tiki

Amazing story about a norwegian explorer, who was interested in demonstrating the possibility that ancient people from South America could have colonized Polynesia.

Lion

A heartbreaking story about a little boy losing his family and getting lost in a big city.

Eat Pray Love

A beautiful story of a womans life, her quest, her ambition and her luck in life.
